Dogs Wearing Sweaters

  1. Dogs do not naturally grow sweaters, they are put on by humans.
  2. Many dogs do not like wearing sweaters, but some do.
  3. Like humans, dogs may require bigger sweaters as they grow up.
Breed of Dog Sweater Size
Chihuahua Extra small
Maltese Small
Golden Retriever Large
